# 🌍 E-Commerce YTD Sales & Profit Analysis (Tableau)

## 📊 Overview
This project analyzes Year-to-Date (YTD) e-commerce sales performance across global markets and product categories. The dashboard provides insights into revenue trends, profitability, order volume, and customer behavior to support data-driven business decisions.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Track YTD sales, profit, and order quantity
- Compare current performance vs previous year (PY)
- Analyze market share distribution across regions
- Identify top-performing and underperforming product categories
- Monitor monthly customer trends across markets

---

## 🛠 Tools & Technologies
- Tableau Desktop Public Edition
- Data Visualization
- Business Intelligence
- KPI Dashboard Design

---

## 📌 Key KPIs
- **Total Sales:** $14.11M (+5.04% vs PY)
- **Total Profit:** $1.30M (-0.46% vs PY)
- **Order Quantity:** 157,978 (+15.02% vs PY)

---

## 📈 Dashboard Components

### 1️⃣ KPI Cards
Displays high-level business metrics:
- Sales growth trend
- Profit fluctuation
- Order volume increase

---

### 2️⃣ Market Share Analysis
- LATAM: 45.86% (highest)
- Europe: 43.37%
- Pacific Asia: 8.32%
- Africa: 2.25%

👉 Insight: LATAM and Europe dominate revenue contribution.

---

### 3️⃣ Category-wise Performance (YTD vs PYTD)
- **Top Category:** Cardio Equipment ($2.34M)
- **High Growth:** Water Sports (+21.10%), Camping & Hiking (+12.55%)
- **Declining Categories:** Fishing (-24.98%), Cleats (-26.34%)

👉 Insight: Outdoor and fitness categories show strong growth, while some traditional categories are declining.

---

### 4️⃣ Monthly Customer Trends (Market-wise)
- Tracks customer activity across months and regions
- Identifies seasonal demand patterns

👉 Insight: Customer engagement varies significantly by region and time, supporting targeted marketing strategies.
